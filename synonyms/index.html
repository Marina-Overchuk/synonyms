Voorbeeldzinnen

Choose a language and enter a word to get examples of how to use it in a sentence.

Have you ever written a work and wanted to use a word you do not know exactly how to use? Then you have found the right place. "Voorbeeldzinnen" is a service that gives you examples of how to use a word. We can also help you find out what it means, find inflections of the word as well as synonyms.
